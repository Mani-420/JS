## üìù Notes

This curriculum is designed to take you from complete beginner to intermediate JavaScript developer. Each lecture builds upon the previous ones, so it's recommended to follow them in order.

**Estimated Time:** 24-48 hours of learning (1-2 hours per lecture)

**Prerequisites:** Basic computer literacy and a willingness to learn!
